Common Questions

How much does a consultation cost?

Nothing. Our consultations are completely free with no obligation. We make money when you choose to move forward with an installation.

What happens during a consultation?

We'll discuss your power outage concerns, current electricity usage, and budget. Then we'll run the actual math on different backup power options for your specific situation.

Do you do high-pressure sales?

No. Our motto is "No pitch. Just math." We'll tell you if backup power doesn't make sense for your situation. We're advisors, not salespeople.

How long until installation?

Typically 6-10 weeks from contract signing. This includes permits, equipment delivery, and scheduling with our installation partners.

What if solar or battery backup doesn't make sense for me?

We'll tell you honestly. Not every home is a good fit‚Äîwhether it's due to shade, roof condition, budget, or timeline. We'd rather lose a sale than have you regret your decision.
