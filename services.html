Solar + Battery Systems

When solar extends your backup runtime and saves money

When Solar Makes Sense

Good Candidates:

  • ✓ South-facing roof with minimal shading
  • ✓ High electricity bills ($150+ monthly)
  • ✓ Want extended backup runtime (5+ days)
  • ✓ Plan to stay in home 7+ years

Poor Candidates:

  • ✗ Heavy tree shading
  • ✗ Low electricity bills (<$100 monthly)
  • ✗ Old roof needing replacement soon
  • ✗ Moving within 5 years

Typical Solar + Battery System

  • Solar Array: 8-15 kW
  • Battery Storage: 20-40 kWh

Duke PowerPair Guidance

Navigate the $9,000 battery incentive program

What is PowerPair?

Duke Energy's incentive program that pays $9,000 for qualifying battery systems. In exchange, Duke can use a small portion of your battery during peak demand periods.

Quick Facts:

  • • $9,000 incentive paid upfront
  • • Continues after federal ITC expires
  • • 10-year program commitment
  • • Limited system control during events
  • • Still maintains backup power for you

PowerPair Requirements

  • ✓ Minimum 7.7 kWh usable battery capacity
  • ✓ Duke Energy customer (most of NC)
  • ✓ Internet connection for monitoring
  • ✓ Professional installation required
